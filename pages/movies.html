Luca

Italy, early 1960s. Luca is a teenage sea monster who discovers the surface after befriending Alberto, another sea monster. Both, coming out of the water, turn into humans. Soon after, Luca is fascinated by a Vespa motorcycle, the freedom to move around the world. After several attempts to create their own motorcycle, Luca and Alberto move to the nearby town of Portorosso, where a competition will be held with the prize of an old scooter.
